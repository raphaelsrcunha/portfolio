About Me

I am a technology and software development enthusiast, currently completing a degree in Software Development at Collège André-Grasset in Montreal. With a solid engineering background from the State University of Campinas, my professional journey is marked by a blend of practical experience and technical knowledge.

In recent years, I have worked extensively in leadership and coordination roles within software engineering. I spent two years as a Scrum Master and more recently served as a Software Engineering Coordinator, where I managed two teams of 10 people each. In this role, I worked with a diverse team, including backend and frontend developers, QAs, Product Managers, a Software Architect, and a QA Lead. This experience has provided me with a comprehensive understanding of development processes and honed my skills in coordinating and leading cross-functional teams.

My passion for computing and software development drives my career. I have specialized in backend development, with a particular focus on Java and Spring, and I am always seeking challenges that allow me to apply and expand my technical expertise.

I am excited about opportunities that allow me to continue growing in the field of software development, especially in environments that value innovation and collaboration. If you are looking for a professional with a strong technical foundation, leadership experience, and a genuine passion for technology, I would be delighted to discuss how I can contribute to the success of your team.
